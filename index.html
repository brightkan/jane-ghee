About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

Jane’s Ghee is culinary gold, excellent cooked butter. Refined manually as well as with fire, it is the most versatile and imperishable oil or Golden Ghee. Its use is universal; greasing, baking, frying, roasting, mixing it with dough, used as a spread, a side dish, for body care and much more. When used daily it helps to maintain well-being. cooked/refined butter, it is known to our great-great grandmothers and was celebrated throughout the territory of present Ankole long before cooling devices came into existence.

What we do

Our Produce Is Mainstay For Us

While people were pondering on how to preserve the best (the essence) of what milk gives us, the imperishable ghee is made over fire. In the countryside it is used to treat joints, frostbite, dry skin, inflamed infant anus, chapped lips, Farmers used Ghee only during festivities. On market stalls, however, it was the most popular and the most expensive food in the great Ankole, and the first to be sold out already in early mornings. Jane’s Ghee is an excellent source of butyric and caprylic acid and is thus traditionally used as a preventive measure and for intestinal problems.

Healthy benefits

Jane’s Ghee is cooked butter. Through an extremely slow cooking process (48 hours) the butter is refined – carbon hydrates, proteins, casein, lactose, impurities and water are removed, leaving only pure fat or oil. Ghee is one of the most body-friendly fats, for it does not contain trans-fatty acids, it is made from natural milk (eco/bio) and does not raise cholesterol levels. It is known to be the secret ingredient of many cooks, who, if not for the whole dish, use Ghee at least to prepare herbs, dressings, side dishes and other supplements. In comparison with raw butter it is incomparably longer lasting and thus suitable for hiking or spending time at the see side. In the refrigerator or in the cellar it can be stored for years. It is also ideal for sweet dishes and a real discovery for those who enjoy making cookies, sponge cakes, muffins and other sweets. With other dishes it is a perfect alternative to supplement for many, if not all, fats. We encourage you to include in your diet several different fats, as the body needs a wide spectrum of elements. The golden-yellow colour, pleasant and full aroma, universal applicability, practicality and exceptional taste, as well as food refinement and nutrients binding, along with revitalization and upkeep of optimal bodily functions, make Jane’s Ghee indispensable in traditional and other quality cuisines. Ghee is known almost all over the world, with roots supposedly originating from great Ankole. It can serve as the basis for herbal concoctions, creams, ointments, massage mixtures, weekly cancer treatments, etc. Example: jane’s ghee mixed with honey, and possibly also with turmeric, heals wounds.
